ZEVOIR TECHNOLOGIES — SERVICES

Zevoir Technologies is an Australian AI and technology company based in Sydney.
We help businesses grow using cutting-edge AI, data, and web technology.

AI CHATBOT DEVELOPMENT
Zevoir builds custom AI chatbots for businesses of all sizes.
Our chatbots are powered by large language models like Claude and GPT-4.
We use RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to make chatbots answer from your own documents.
Chatbots can be embedded on any website or app.
Use cases include: customer support, lead capture, internal knowledge base, appointment booking.
Chatbots are available 24/7 and handle thousands of conversations simultaneously.

AI AUTOMATION
We automate repetitive business tasks using AI.
Examples: automated email responses, document processing, data entry, report generation.
AI automation saves businesses an average of 20-30 hours per week.
We integrate with existing tools like Slack, Gmail, CRMs, and ERPs.

DATA ANALYTICS AND DASHBOARDS
Zevoir builds interactive dashboards showing real-time business insights.
We connect to your existing databases and data sources.
Dashboards include charts, KPIs, trend analysis, and predictive analytics.

Data analytics helps businesses make faster, smarter decisions.

WEB AND APP DEVELOPMENT

CLOUD SOLUTIONS

AI SECURITY AND GOVERNANCE
