ZEVOIR TECHNOLOGIES — SUPPORT INFORMATION

CONTACT AND SUPPORT
Email: support@zevoir.com.au
Phone: 1800-ZEVOIR (business hours: Mon-Fri 9am-5pm AEST)
Live chat: available on our website and through this chatbot
Response time: within 24 hours on business days

ACCOUNT ISSUES
If you cannot log in to your Zevoir account:
1. Try the Forgot Password option on the login page
2. Check your email for a reset link (check spam folder too)
3. Reset links expire after 15 minutes — request a new one if needed
4. If your account is locked after multiple failed attempts, contact support
5. For urgent account issues call 1800-ZEVOIR

OTP AND VERIFICATION
If you did not receive your OTP (one-time password):
1. Wait 30 seconds — OTPs can sometimes be delayed
2. Check you entered the correct phone number on your account
3. Check your spam/junk folder if it was sent by email
4. Request a new OTP using the Resend button
5. If the issue continues, contact our support team

ORDER AND PROJECT STATUS
To check the status of your project or order:
1. Log in to your Zevoir client portal
2. Go to Projects or Orders section
3. Each project shows current status: Scoping, In Progress, Review, Complete
4. You will receive email updates at each milestone
5. For urgent queries contact your dedicated account manager

TECHNICAL SUPPORT
For technical issues with Zevoir products:
1. Check our documentation at docs.zevoir.com.au
2. Search our FAQ for common issues
3. Submit a support ticket through the client portal
4. For critical issues (system down) call 1800-ZEVOIR immediately

REFUNDS AND CANCELLATIONS
Refund policy:
- Projects cancelled before work begins: full refund of deposit
- Projects cancelled after work begins: refund of unused portion
- Monthly retainers: cancel anytime with 30 days notice
- Digital products delivered: no refund once delivered and accepted

ESCALATION
If your issue is not resolved by our support team:
1. Ask to speak with a senior support specialist
2. Email escalations@zevoir.com.au
3. Request a callback from your account manager
We aim to resolve all escalations within 48 hours.
