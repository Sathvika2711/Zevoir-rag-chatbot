ZEVOIR TECHNOLOGIES — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What does Zevoir Technologies do?
A: Zevoir Technologies is a Sydney-based AI and technology company. We build AI chatbots, data analytics dashboards, web applications, and cloud solutions to help businesses grow and automate their operations.

Q: Where is Zevoir Technologies located?
A: Zevoir Technologies is based in Sydney, Australia. We work with clients across Australia and internationally.

Q: How long does it take to build a chatbot?
A: A basic chatbot takes 1-2 weeks. A full RAG-powered chatbot with custom documents takes 2-4 weeks. Enterprise solutions are scoped individually.

Q: Can you build a chatbot for my specific business?
A: Yes. All our chatbots are custom-built for each client. We train them on your own documents, FAQs, product information, and business data.

Q: What AI models do you use?
A: We primarily use Claude by Anthropic and GPT-4 by OpenAI. We recommend the best model based on your use case, budget, and data privacy requirements.

Q: What is RAG?
A: RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Instead of relying on the AI's general knowledge, RAG searches your own documents and feeds the relevant information to the AI before it answers. This means answers are grounded in your actual business data, not guesswork.
